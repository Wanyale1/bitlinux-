BitLinux FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? +

To create an account, go to the sign-up page, fill in your details, and click "Create Account".

Can I host my website for free? +

Yes! You can connect your GitHub account and deploy websites to Vercel using our hosting page.

How do I reset my password? +

If you forget your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?" to reset it via email.

What programming resources are available? +

Our Learn Programming page provides downloadable PDFs and tutorials for web development and coding.
